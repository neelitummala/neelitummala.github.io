I'm a postdoctoral researcher at Northwestern University's Center for Robotics and Biosystems (CRB) in the SeNSE Lab.

Previously, I was a Ph.D. candidate in the electrical and computer engineering department at UC Santa Barbara and a graduate student researcher in the RE Touch Lab. The goal of my research was to better understand how our tactile system encodes information from our environment and to use that knowledge for creating better haptic devices, robots, and prosthetics, as well as for developing tools and methods to aid people with sensory disorders.
