I'm a PhD student in the ECE department at UC Santa Barbara and a graduate student researcher in the ReTouch Lab. The goal of my research is to better understand how our tactile system encodes information from our environment and to use that knowledge for creating better haptic devices, robots, and prosthetics, as well as for developing tools and methods to aid people with sensory disorders.
